From Rookie to Pro-The Rise of Jonathan

Jonathan Amaral, popularly known as Jonathan Gaming, is a renowned Indian BGMI eSports player famous for his "zero recoil" mastery. Starting with PUBG Mobile, he rose to fame with Entity Gaming, showcasing exceptional skills in tournaments like PMCO Fall Split 2019. His aggressive gameplay and clutch performances earned him MVP titles, and he continued his dominance with TSM-Entity. After PUBG's ban, he transitioned to BGMI, joining GodLike Esports.

Breaking Records, Winning Hearts

Jonathan Gaming has an impressive list of achievements in the eSports world. He was the MVP of PMIS 2020 and consistently top-fragged in tournaments like PMCO Fall Split 2019, where his team secured top positions. Known for his "zero recoil" sprays, he holds records for the highest kill counts in multiple BGMI events. With TSM-Entity, he dominated the Indian scene, and after joining GodLike Esports, he continued to shine in BGIS 2021, securing top placements. Jonathan is also the first Indian player to cross 100 MVP titles in official tournaments, solidifying his legacy in mobile gaming.

More Than a Player-A Legacy in the Making

Jonathan Gaming is more than just a player; he’s a legacy in the making. His journey from casual gaming to becoming one of India’s top BGMI eSports athletes showcases his unmatched skill, dedication, and passion. Known for his iconic "zero recoil" sprays and clutch performances, Jonathan has redefined mobile gaming standards in India. His impact extends beyond the battlefield, shaping the future of Indian eSports. As he continues to break records and set new benchmarks, Jonathan’s legacy as a gaming legend is just beginning.

GodLike Esports: Dominating the Indian eSports Scene

GodLike Esports has become a household name in the Indian eSports scene, known for its exceptional gameplay and dominance in titles like BGMI. Founded with the vision of creating a powerhouse, the team quickly rose to fame with top-tier players like Jonathan, ClutchGod, and Neyoo. Over the years, GodLike has consistently outperformed in national tournaments, earning a reputation for strategic gameplay, teamwork, and raw talent. Their success not only secured championships but also played a crucial role in elevating Indian eSports on the global map. Today, GodLike stands as a symbol of excellence, inspiring future generations of gamers across India.
